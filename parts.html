Terms and Conditions

What we accept

We currently accept parts in good working condition, unless agreed otherwise, including but not limited too:

  • CPUs
  • GPUs
  • RAM
  • SSDs/HHDs
  • Motherboards
  • Power Supplies
  • Laptops
  • Phones
  • PCs

We do not accept

  • Water Damaged Items - Unless agreed otherwise
  • Items with missing components (E.G. A GPU with a missing fan) - Unless agreed otherwise
  • Broken or non-functional parts - Unless agreed beforehand

Sending Your Items

  • You are responible for shipping the item to us, safely and securely
  • We recommend you use tracked delivery with suitable packaging, email us if you need help with packaging.
  • Parcli PCs is not responsible for any items lost or damaged during transit. It's the sellers responsibilty to ensure that parts are packaged securly and sent with a reliable delivery service

Make sure we have the correct payment details and contact information via email when arranging the sale.

Testing Process

Once we recieve your item:

  • We will inspect and test it within 2-5 working days.
  • If the item works as described and in the expected condition, we will send you payment via your chosen method.
  • If the item is faulty or significantly different to what was agreed, we will:
    • Contact you Immediatly
    • Offer a return (we cover return postage), or
    • Make a lower offer, which you can accept or reject.

Payments

  • Payments are usually sent within 2-5 working days of us recieving and testing your part.
  • We currently offer payment by:
    • UK Bank Transfer

Returns

If there is an issue and we cannot accept your part:

  • We will return the item at no cost to you, unless otherwise agreed
  • Items will be returned to the address provided (you must ensure it is correct - It is not our responsability).

Fraud Preventation

To protect both sides:

  • We keep full records of items recieved, tested and returned.
  • If we recieve a different item from what was agreed, or if fraud is suspected, we reserve the right to refuse the transaction.
