A Passion For Technology. A Passion For The Environment.

At Parcli, we know that manufacturing new technology and PC parts can have a high carbon footprint. Because of this, we incourage you to buy used parts and stop them from going to waste.

Old Tech Doesn't Need To Be Thrown Out

Despite an old computer feeling slow and old, like it will never work properly again, this may not be the case. For example, one of our co-founders saved an old, slow laptop from going to the dump, and after a RAM upgrade and switching the software to chrome OS, as it runs better on old devices, the laptop worked fine.
